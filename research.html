Adaptive VR Interfaces for Elderly Healthcare Monitoring

Platform: Zenodo — 10.5281/zenodo.17790985

This paper explores barriers elderly users face in VR and proposes interface modifications that reduce cognitive load and increase confidence. Includes user testing, recommendations and prototype evaluation.
